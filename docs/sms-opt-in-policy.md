# SMS Opt-In & Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## What You're Signing Up For

By texting our business number or replying "YES" to opt in, you agree to receive SMS messages from us including:

- Appointment confirmations and reminders
- Responses to your questions about our services
- Occasional promotions or special offers (you can opt out anytime)

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. You may receive:
- Immediate responses when you text us
- Appointment reminders (typically 24 hours before)
- Occasional follow-ups or promotions (no more than 4 per month)

## Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

## How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:
- Replying **STOP** to any message
- Texting **STOP** to our business number
- Contacting us directly and asking to be removed

After opting out, you'll receive one final confirmation message. You will not receive any more messages unless you opt back in.

## How to Get Help

For help or questions about our SMS service:
- Reply **HELP** to any message
- Contact us directly at our business number

## Your Privacy

We respect your privacy and will never:
- Sell your phone number to third parties
- Share your information with other businesses
- Send messages unrelated to our services

We only collect:
- Your phone number
- Your name (if you provide it)
- Your conversation history with us
- Appointment details you schedule

This information is used solely to provide you with better service.

## Consent

By texting us first or replying "YES" to an opt-in message, you provide express written consent to receive SMS messages from us at the phone number you used to text us.

## Supported Carriers

Our SMS service works with all major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please text us or contact us directly.
